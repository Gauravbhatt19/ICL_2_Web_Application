IHET CRICKET LEAGUE

Season-1

About League

A Cricket League is being organised by the Sports Department,THDC-IHET. This League is being organised for purpose of amusement as well as for providing a platform to upcoming players to showcase their skills in the field of Cricket so that they can provide valuable contributions to the college in the upcoming UTU Cricket Tournament. The tournament shall be held at the college cricket ground from 7th of Sept'18 to 18th Sept'18. All the matches shall be played with Cricket Tennis Ball.It is planned to register 114+ players and auction will decide final 8 teams.
