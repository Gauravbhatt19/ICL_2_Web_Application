About League

A Cricket League is being organised by the Hydro Cricket Club. This League is being organised for purpose of amusement as well as for providing a platform to upcoming players to showcase their skills in the field of Cricket. The tournament shall be held at the college cricket ground from 17th of Feb'19. All the matches shall be played with Cricket Tennis Ball.It is planned to register 114+ players and auction will decide final 10 teams.
